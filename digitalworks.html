Most of these were made during my first year of owning an iPad, which I was proud of because I bought it with my own money. While I initially have more knowledge with using Paint Tool Sai on a PC with a Wacom attached to my monitor, I was more excited about using an iPad because i was more used to the pen-and-paper approach of drawing things. I used Procreate on my iPad to create the works below, most of which I made in the first year I bought my beloved device with my hard-earned money.

Abstract Works

These works were inspired by my Art 74 class when I learned about Glitch Art through Audcity. I loved the way glitch was, in a way, an aesthetic. Finding beauty in error and making it work was my thing. I decided to play with this idea and found out that I can use the tools "Liquify", "Glitch", and "Burn" to alter my artwork to make it look abstract and "glitch-y". This then inspired me to implement the artworks below in a student work series for my Art 74 final called "Dreams of the Subcoscious".
