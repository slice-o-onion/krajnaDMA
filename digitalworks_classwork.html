My inspiration draws from weird bulletin boards advertising their business using weird images. The images usually tend to use many old-school type of designs (such as over-multiplying the images) that make the viewer question what they just saw while driving on the highway. The images are taken by me personally during a photoshoot of me and my friend way back in highschool, and the photos are so reusable as something I can always utilize whenever I want to use images of my own that can spark interest.
